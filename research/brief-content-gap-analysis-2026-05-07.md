## 2. Competitive Landscape

### Content Gaps (Our Opportunity)
1. **No ROI framing**: Competitors explain what gap analysis is; nobody quantifies what closing gaps is worth
2. **Qualification framework missing**: All guides say "find gaps" -- none explain which gaps to prioritize or skip
3. **Agency POV absent**: Every guide is written for DIY marketers with tools access -- no "here's how an agency does this for clients"
4. **Common mistakes section thin**: Competitors focus on the how, not the failure modes

### Differentiation Strategy
Lead with the "pre-validated demand" angle -- gap analysis is faster than traditional keyword research because competitors have proven the market. Provide a concrete prioritization formula (not just "look at volume and difficulty"). Use a mini-story to anchor the problem before the solution.
