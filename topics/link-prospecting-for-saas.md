## Initial Thoughts
- Broad "link prospecting" guides exist, but virtually none are SaaS-specific
- SaaS link prospecting has distinct contexts: integration marketplaces, tool directories, SaaS review sites (G2, Capterra), niche newsletters
- Opportunity: most "how to find link prospects" guides focus on guest posting outreach -- ignoring the passive link sources unique to SaaS products
- This bridges research (finding prospects) with the tactics in the pillar (earning the links)
