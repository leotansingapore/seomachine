# Topic: SEO Reporting

## Cluster
Cluster 5 — SEO Reporting & Analytics (PILLAR)

## Core Idea
Most SEO reports show traffic and rankings. That's not enough. This guide covers how to build reports that connect SEO actions to business outcomes — revenue, pipeline, and ROI — for three audiences: executives, SEO teams, and clients.

## Differentiation Angle
The "3-layer framework" (Executive / Team / Client) — matching report depth to audience.  
Most guides treat all SEO reports the same. The gap: nobody covers how to have the "traffic is down but revenue is up" conversation, or how to include AI citation visibility as a modern metric.
