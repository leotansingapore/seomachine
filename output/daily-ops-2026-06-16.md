**Primary keyword**: conversion rate optimization for SaaS (~1,500/mo, KD: ~45)  
**Cluster**: Cluster 9 — Conversion Rate Optimization (PILLAR)

**Five competitor gaps identified:**
1. No SEO-to-conversion intent mapping — all CRO guides assume paid traffic; zero content connects organic search intent tiers to CRO strategy
2. No self-serve vs. sales-assisted distinction — PLG and SLG CRO tactics differ completely but no guide separates them cleanly
3. No buying committee framework — every B2C CRO tactic assumes one decision-maker; B2B SaaS involves 6.8 stakeholders (Gartner, 2025)
4. No pipeline yield framing — CRO content measures micro-conversions in isolation; no article calculates end-to-end organic visitor → MQL → trial → paid → expansion
5. No content-tier CTA strategy — the right CTA depends on buyer stage; most guides assume one CTA fits all content types

**Differentiating angle**: CRO for B2B SaaS is four problems stacked, not one. Fix them in sequence: intent conversion (traffic quality), page conversion (classic CRO), journey conversion (CTA matching), pipeline conversion (trial/demo to paid). Most teams only address problem two.
