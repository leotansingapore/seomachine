**Cluster**: Cluster 5 — SEO Reporting & Analytics (Supporting #3)  

**Key gaps identified in competitor content**:
1. Every competitor guide covers "how to add keywords to the tool" — none explains the setup decisions that determine whether tracking is signal or noise (architecture, keyword selection criteria, device/location targeting, cadence)
2. No guide covers the tracking architecture concept — tracking by page group vs. flat keyword list
3. No normal-fluctuation framework — competitors don't tell you what ±1–3 positions means (ignore) vs. 8+ positions (act)
4. No workflow integration — rank data is always presented in isolation from GSC and GA4
5. No "what to remove" guidance — all guides focus on adding more keywords, never on cleaning up noise
6. No free-first path — most guides are written by tool companies promoting their own trackers

**Differentiating angle**: The 4 setup decisions before adding a single keyword — tracking architecture, keyword selection, device/location targeting, and cadence — plus a signal vs. noise decision framework and an integrated Monday review workflow connecting rank data to GSC and GA4. Also unique: a "what to remove from your tracker" section that no competitor covers.
