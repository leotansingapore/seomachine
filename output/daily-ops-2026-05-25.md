**File**: `research/brief-seo-reporting-2026-05-25.md`  
**Primary keyword**: SEO reporting (~2,400/mo, KD: Medium ~38)  
**Cluster**: Cluster 5 — SEO Reporting & Analytics (pillar article)

**Key gaps identified in competitor content**:
1. No stakeholder segmentation — every competitor writes "the SEO report" as a single document; nobody addresses that executives, teams, and clients need fundamentally different artifacts
2. No GA4 migration coverage — most guides predate or ignore the UA→GA4 attribution break
3. No AI search visibility — zero competitors cover AI Overview appearances or Perplexity citations as a measurable SEO metric
4. No "traffic down" reporting framework — the most-used skill in agency reporting, completely absent from all competitor guides
5. No revenue translation formula — guides list organic traffic as a KPI; nobody shows the math for connecting rankings to revenue estimates

**Differentiation angle**: The "3-layer reporting framework" (Executive 1-pager / Team dashboard / Client narrative) — matching report depth and format to audience. This is the organizing hook no competitor uses.
