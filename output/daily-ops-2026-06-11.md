**Cluster**: Cluster 8 — E-E-A-T and Authority Building (Supporting #1)

**Five competitor gaps identified:**
1. No buyer journey mapping — all existing guides treat topical authority as an SEO-only concern, ignoring that B2B buying cycles span 6–18 months across multi-stakeholder committees
2. No multi-stakeholder framework — champion, economic buyer, technical evaluator, and blocker each search differently; no guide addresses this content coverage requirement
3. No revenue/pipeline connection — all metrics are rankings/impressions-only; no B2B guide connects topical authority to influenced pipeline or deal velocity

5. No measurement framework beyond rankings — branded search growth, cluster ranking coverage %, SERP presence rate, citation rate, influenced pipeline % all absent

**Differentiating angle**: Topical authority for B2B SaaS is buyer trust infrastructure, not a content volume goal. When every stakeholder in a buying committee independently finds your content authoritative, the sales conversation starts from established trust. Frame as a four-layer stack (search coverage → buyer journey depth → multi-stakeholder coverage → authority signals) with a buyer stage × stakeholder content matrix.
